Odd Drawer



Nine test tubes are placed in a row on the drawer.
Namely: (Not in this order though)
H2O, HCl, NaOH, HI, HNO3, HBr, H2SO4, HF, H2S.

Deduce the correct order from the given facts about them and write the correct arrangement of the test-tubes:

  1. HNO3 and H2SO4 are next to exactly one test tube
  2. There are 2 test-tubes between NaOH and H2O
  3. HBr is between H2S and HCl
  4. HCl is immediately right to HNO3
  5. NaOH is immediately left to H2SO4
  6. NaOH is immediately right to HF
  7. HI is immediately right to H2O


Note: Write answer as comma separated without any space in between;
e.g.: A,B,C
