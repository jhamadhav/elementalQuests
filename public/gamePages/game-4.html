  1. Every appearing mineral has amount/count written on it's face that is the amount of mineral you will gain.

  4. Collecting more than given amount will result in incorrect attempt.
  5. When Mining, minerals might stick together, beware !
  6. The value that appears on the face of minerals follows the octet sequence seen in game 1.
  7. If you find yourself at a dead-end, you can refer back to the relevant game information.
    Click to visit "Octet Lock"(game-1)
  8. But remember the clock is ticking.
